## Purpose

This document defines data retention expectations for platform data, including operational and user-generated content.

## Strategy

- Retain data according to legal and business requirements
- Separate short-lived operational data from long-term business data
- Apply deletion and archival policies consistently
- Maintain auditability for retention decisions
